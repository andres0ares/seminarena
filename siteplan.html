Overview

Purpose

To inform people of the Dry Oar, how they can participate in its marvelous experience alone or with their families during a holiday or vacation. The Dry Oar gives a new activity in a radical sport with security and enthusiasm.

Audience

It includes teenagers and adults of all ages that want to participate in the Dry Oar alone or with their families during a holiday or vacation. They want to try new experiences with radical sports with safety and excitement. It also includes athletes and professionals that want to practice and have fun. They have a stable financial situation and want to spend money with themselves and or with their families.The old public will access the site using the desktop. The young adults and the teenagers will access using the laptop and or the mobile device.

Wireframe

The site will contain five sections: header, testemonies, activities, scriptures and a footer.

The fist one, the header will display a logo, a navbar and a illustration.

The testemonies section will follow the header section, it will contain three cards with the testemonies of students about the seminary and the gospel of Jesus Christ.

In the Activities section, the student will find intructions and informations about the activities of the week and month.

The scriptures section will display a passage of scripitures that the students are studying this year.

Last, the footer will contain some contact info and the credits.
